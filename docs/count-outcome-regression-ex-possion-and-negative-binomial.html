8.3.2.5 Interpretation

  • female: Adjusting for the effects of rate/ethnicity, education, and income, FEMALES are expected to volunteer about 30% MORE activities per year than males.

  • nonwhite: Adjusting for the effects of sex, education, and income, NON-WHITES are expected to volunteer for about 24% LESS activities per year than males.

  • educate: Each one-year increase in education is associated with an 11% increase in the number of volunteer activities per year, adjusting for the effects of sex, race/ethnicity, and income.
